Projects

Rongai Children Charity

Poster design, Typography

A set of posters for talk events, discussing volunteering opportunities for the charity RCC. Concept derived from the notion of missing letters as a metaphor for desired volunteers. It is derived from visual experimentation with letterpress.

Manhole covers typology

Poster design, Information design

An A2 poster, a collection of manhole covers from the city of Westminster. The covers are classified according to general type, and highlight the wide the array of designs and use of geometric shapes.

Stanford Maps events

Poster design

A set of 6 A1 posters designed to promote a series of talks held at the Stanford Maps London store. The concept is centred around the aesthetics of maps.

Aphorisms

Postcard design, Typography

A set of Aphorisms by the philosopher Ralph Waldo Emerson. The set of A5 cards are produced through various printmaking techniques, using a variety of typefaces, carefully selected to help convey the meaning of the key words in the quotes.

Urban Abstraction

Book design

A 34-page, A3 publication Exploring Elements of De Stijl in London's urban environment. The book takes the reader through a journey of shape distillation, in accordance with the rules set out in the movement's manifesto

Visual summary

Book design, Typography

A 42-page, A4 book of my research and experimentation of design fundamentals such as typography, visual grammar and information design. It showcases highlights from my sketchbooks, and details sources of inspiration informing my experimentation and development.
